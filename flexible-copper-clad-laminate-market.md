<p><p>Flexible Copper-Clad Laminate market offers various types of production processes, such as tape casting, sputtering, electroplating, and laminating. Tape casting involves spreading a paste onto a substrate and drying it to form a thin film. Sputtering uses a physical vapor deposition process to deposit thin films onto a substrate. Electroplating involves the deposition of a metal coating onto a conductive surface using an electric current. Laminating involves bonding multiple layers of materials together to create a flexible copper-clad laminate.</p></p>
