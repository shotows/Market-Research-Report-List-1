<p><strong>The Protein Purification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrafiltration</li><li>Precipitation</li><li>Chromatography</li><li>Electrophoresis</li><li>Western Blotting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protein purification market involves various methods such as ultrafiltration, precipitation, chromatography, electrophoresis, western blotting, and others. Ultrafiltration utilizes membrane filters to separate proteins based on size, while precipitation uses chemicals to isolate proteins. Chromatography involves separating proteins based on their properties and interactions with a stationary phase. Electrophoresis separates proteins based on their charge and size. Western blotting detects and quantifies specific proteins in a sample. Other methods include crystallization, dialysis, and centrifugation. These techniques are essential for isolating and characterizing proteins in research and biopharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
