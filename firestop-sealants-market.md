<p><strong>The Firestop Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastomeric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Firestop sealants are important in preventing the spread of fire in buildings by sealing gaps and penetrations. There are two main types of firestop sealants on the market: Elastomeric Type and Intumescent Type. Elastomeric sealants remain flexible even after curing, providing a durable seal against fire and smoke. Intumescent sealants expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming a char barrier that helps prevent the spread of fire. Both types play a crucial role in fire safety in buildings.</p></p>
