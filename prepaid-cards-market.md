<p><p>Prepaid cards are categorized into single-purpose and multi-purpose cards. Single-purpose prepaid cards are designed for specific uses, such as gift cards or transit cards, limiting their functionality to a particular product or service. They offer convenience and control but lack flexibility. On the other hand, multi-purpose prepaid cards allow users to load funds for various purposes like shopping, bill payments, or ATM withdrawals. These cards offer versatility, as they can be used at any merchant that accepts card payments, providing more freedom and convenience for users.</p></p>
