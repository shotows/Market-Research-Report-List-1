<p><ul><li>On-demand</li><li>Prophylaxis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemophilia treatment market includes two main types: on-demand treatment and prophylaxis treatment. On-demand treatment is used when a bleeding episode occurs, and it involves administering clotting factor concentrates to stop the bleeding. Prophylaxis treatment, on the other hand, is a preventive approach that involves regular infusions of clotting factor concentrates to avoid or reduce the frequency of bleeding episodes. Both types aim to manage and control the symptoms of hemophilia, but they differ in their timing and frequency of treatment administration.</p></p>
