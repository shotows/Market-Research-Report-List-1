<p><p>The Antilock Brake System (ABS) market can be categorized into four types based on the number of channels: Four Channel ABS, Three Channel ABS, Two Channel ABS, and One Channel ABS. Four Channel ABS operates independently on all four wheels, providing the highest level of control and safety. Three Channel ABS works on three wheels, typically found in older vehicles. Two Channel ABS operates on two wheels, while One Channel ABS operates on only one wheel, offering the least amount of control and safety.</p></p>
