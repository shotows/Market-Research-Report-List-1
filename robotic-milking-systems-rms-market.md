<p><p>Robotic Milking Systems (RMS) come in three main market types: Standalone Units, Multiple Stall Units, and Rotary Units. Standalone Units are individual machines that operate independently. Multiple Stall Units can milk several cows at once, increasing efficiency. Rotary Units are circular setups where cows move along a platform while being milked. Each market type offers unique advantages for dairy farmers, with Rotary Units typically being the most efficient for larger herds. RMS technology continues to evolve to meet the diverse needs of the dairy industry.</p></p>
