<p><p>Technical Grade Glycerin is typically used in industrial applications such as in the production of chemicals, plastics, and explosives. It is not intended for human consumption and may contain impurities. On the other hand, USP Grade Glycerin is a highly purified form that meets the standards set by the United States Pharmacopeia.
