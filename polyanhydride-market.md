<p><p>Polyanhydrides are biodegradable polymers used in various industries due to their unique properties. Two common types are Poly(Sebacic Anhydride) (PSAn) and Poly(Azelaic Anhydride) (PAAn). PSAn is known for its flexibility and toughness, making it suitable for drug delivery systems. PAAn, on the other hand, is valued for its hydrophobic nature and controlled drug release capabilities. Both polymers are widely used in the medical field for developing implantable devices and drug delivery systems due to their biocompatibility and biodegradability.</p></p>
