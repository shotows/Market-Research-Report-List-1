Automatic seeders are equipped with advanced technology to automatically dispense seeds at a consistent rate. Semi-automatic seeders require some manual assistance, such as adjusting the seed depth or spacing. Manual seeders rely entirely on human power to operate, making them the most labor-intensive option.
