LCOS is widely used in projectors, head-mounted displays, and near-eye displays.</p><p>The global LCOS market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% during the forecast period. Swept Volume Volumetric Display creates a 3D image by rapidly scanning a 2D image across a volume, creating the illusion of depth. Static Volume Volumetric Display uses multiple flat panel displays to create 3D images. Multiplanar Volumetric Display uses multiple layers of LCOS panels to achieve depth perception. These technologies enhance visual experiences by creating lifelike 3D images.</p></p>
