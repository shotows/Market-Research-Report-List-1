<p><p>The Fiberglass Piping System market includes various types of pipes such as GRE Pipes, GRP Pipes, and others. GRE Pipes, also known as Glass Reinforced Epoxy Pipes, are made of epoxy resin and glass fibers, offering high corrosion resistance and strength. GRP Pipes, or Glass Reinforced Plastic Pipes, are made of thermosetting polyester resin and glass fibers, providing durability and flexibility. Other types in the market may include composite pipes with different combinations of materials like carbon fiber or kevlar for specific applications.</p></p>
