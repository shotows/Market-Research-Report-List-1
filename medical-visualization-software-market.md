<p><p>Medical visualization software is categorized into different types, including image post-processing systems and image annotation systems. Image post-processing systems analyze and enhance medical images, such as X-rays or MRIs, by applying filters, adjusting contrast, and removing noise. In contrast, image annotation systems assist in marking specific features, structures, or abnormalities within medical images for better diagnosis and communication among medical professionals. Both types of software play crucial roles in improving medical imaging quality, interpretation, and collaboration to deliver more accurate and effective patient care.</p></p>
