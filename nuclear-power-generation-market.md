<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR)</li><li>Boiler Water Reactor (BWR)</li><li>Pressurized Heavy Water Reactor (PHWR)</li><li>Gas Cooled Reactor (GCR)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear power generation market encompasses various reactor types. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) employ uranium fuel, using water as both coolant and moderator. Boiler Water Reactors (BWR) operate with slightly lower pressure and let boiling water create steam for electricity generation. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) use heavy water as a moderator and coolant, while natural uranium fuel is employed. Gas Cooled Reactors (GCR) use carbon dioxide to transfer heat from fuel to a gas turbine for power production. "Others" represent miscellaneous reactor types, not falling into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
