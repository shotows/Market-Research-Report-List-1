<p><p>Cosmetic antimicrobial agents are used to prevent the growth of bacteria, fungi, and other microorganisms in cosmetic products. Paraben Esters are commonly used preservatives, while Formaldehyde Donors release formaldehyde to inhibit microbial growth.
