<p><p>Solvent-borne coil coatings are used in various markets including primer coatings, back coatings, and top coatings. Primer coatings are applied as the first layer to enhance adhesion and provide corrosion protection. Back coatings are applied to the reverse side of the coil to provide additional protection. Top coatings are the final layer applied to provide aesthetic appeal and further protect the surface. Each type of coating plays a crucial role in enhancing the durability and performance of the coated surface.</p></p>
