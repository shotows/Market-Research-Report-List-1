<p><p>Circular Polarized Antennas market consists of two main types: Left-Handed Circular Polarization (LHCP) and Right-Handed Circular Polarization (RHCP). LHCP antennas produce a rotating electric field in a counterclockwise direction, while RHCP antennas produce a rotating electric field in a clockwise direction. Both types are used in various applications such as satellite communication, radio astronomy, and wireless communication systems. The choice between LHCP and RHCP antennas depends on the specific requirements of the application and the compatibility of the equipment being used.</p></p>
