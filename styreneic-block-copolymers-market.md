<p><p>Styreneic block copolymers, which are versatile materials widely used in various industries, can be categorized into three main types: SEBS (styrene-ethylene-butylene-styrene), SIS (styrene-isoprene-styrene), and other markets. SEBS copolymers are known for their excellent resistance to weathering and flexibility, making them ideal for applications in the automotive and medical sectors. SIS copolymers, on the other hand, offer high adhesion and elasticity properties, making them suitable for use in adhesives and sealants. Other markets for styreneic block copolymers include construction, electronics, and personal care products.</p></p>
