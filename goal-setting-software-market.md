<p><p>Goal setting software is available in two different market types, on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and operated on the organization's own servers, requiring maintenance and updates by the IT department. It offers higher data security but limited access and scalability. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. It provides greater flexibility, scalability, and accessibility since it can be accessed from any device with an internet connection. However, it might be perceived as less secure, and organizations rely on the provider for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
