<p><p>Lead Salt PVC stabilizers can be classified into two types: monomer lead salt stabilizers and composite lead salt stabilizers. Monomer lead salt stabilizers are single chemical compounds that actively interact with PVC to prevent degradation. On the other hand, composite lead salt stabilizers are blends of various chemicals that work together to stabilize PVC. Both types of stabilizers play a crucial role in enhancing the heat stability and durability of PVC products in various applications.</p></p>
