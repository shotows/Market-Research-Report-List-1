<p><p>Membrane chromatography consumables market types include various products used in chromatography processes. Syringe filters are small cartridges with a membrane that removes impurities from samples. Filter plates consist of a rigid structure with multiple wells that hold membranes for sample filtration. Spin columns are small tubes with a membrane at the bottom, allowing for separation of desired components. Membrane discs and sheets are commonly used for preparative purposes. Other market types encompass a range of consumables that aid in chromatography, such as filter holders, membrane cassettes, and cartridges. These consumables are critical for efficient and accurate chromatographic separation.</p></p>
