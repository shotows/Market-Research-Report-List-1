<p><strong>The Thermal Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal printers utilize heat to print on paper. There are two main types in the market. Direct thermal printers use heat-sensitive paper that blackens when heat is applied, eliminating the need for ink or toner. This makes them suitable for printing receipts, labels, and tickets. Thermal transfer printers, on the other hand, use heat to transfer ink from a ribbon onto the paper, producing more durable and long-lasting prints. They are commonly used for product labeling, barcode printing, and signage.</p></p>
