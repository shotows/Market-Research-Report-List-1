<p><p>Semi-fluid lubricants can be categorized into three main types: Mineral oil-based, semisynthetic oil-based, and other formulations. Mineral oil-based lubricants are derived from petroleum and are known for their excellent heat resistance. Semisynthetic oil-based lubes are a blend of mineral and synthetic oils, offering enhanced performance properties. Other types of semi-fluid lubricants may include bio-based or specialty formulations designed for specific applications. Each type of lubricant offers unique benefits and characteristics to meet the varying needs of different industries and equipment.</p></p>
