<p><p>HDPE bottles can be categorized into two main types based on their appearance: translucent and opaque. Translucent HDPE bottles are semi-transparent, allowing some light to pass through, and are often used for products where visibility of the contents is important, such as beverages or beauty products. On the other hand, opaque HDPE bottles are completely solid and do not let light pass through, making them ideal for products that need to be protected from light exposure, such as pharmaceuticals or chemicals. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
