<p><p>Myopic eye drop, also known as myopia eye drop or nearsightedness eye drop, refers to eye drops that are specifically formulated to help manage and correct myopia, a common refractive error. Myopia is characterized by the ability to see nearby objects clearly while distant objects appear blurry. Myopic eye drops work by temporarily altering the shape of the cornea or by relaxing the eye muscles, enabling better focus and vision at a distance.</p><p>The market for myopic eye drops has been witnessing significant growth in recent years due to the increasing prevalence of myopia worldwide. Factors such as the changing lifestyle habits, increased exposure to electronic devices, and genetic factors are contributing to the rise in myopia cases, thereby driving the demand for myopic eye drops.</p><p>The myopic eye drop market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period.
