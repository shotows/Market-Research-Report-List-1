<p><p>Business Spend Management (BSM) Software is categorized into three main types based on the nature of spending: indirect spending, direct spending, and MRO (maintenance, repair, and operations) spending. Indirect spending refers to expenses incurred for the operation of a business such as marketing, IT, and office supplies. Direct spending involves costs related to the production of goods or services, like raw materials and labor. MRO spending covers expenses for the maintenance and repair of equipment and facilities. BSM software helps businesses effectively manage and optimize spending across these different categories.</p></p>
