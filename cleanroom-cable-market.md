One such trend is the increasing adoption of fiber optic cleanroom cables. <p><p>Cleanroom cable market types include single-core cables and multi-core cables. </p><p>Single-core cables are designed with a single conductor, typically wrapped with an insulating material to prevent electrical interference. These cables are commonly used for low-voltage applications, where a single signal or current needs to be transferred.</p><p>On the other hand, multi-core cables consist of multiple insulated conductors that are bundled together. These cables are suitable for applications that require multiple signals or currents to be transmitted simultaneously. They offer more flexibility and enable efficient organization of wires in complex systems.</p></p>
