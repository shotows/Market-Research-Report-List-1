<p><p>Threaded tees are a type of fittings used in piping systems to create branch connections. There are three main types of threaded tees available in the market: Threaded Fitting Tee, Threaded Equal Tee, and Threaded Unequal Tee. A Threaded Fitting Tee has all three ends the same size, while a Threaded Equal Tee has three ends of different sizes. A Threaded Unequal Tee has one outlet smaller than the other two. These tees are commonly used in plumbing, HVAC, and industrial applications.</p></p>
