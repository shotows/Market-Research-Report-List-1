<p><p>Caulking guns come in three main types: Manual, Pneumatic, and Electric. Manual caulking guns are operated by hand and require physical strength to dispense caulk. Pneumatic caulking guns use compressed air to apply caulk at a consistent rate, making them faster and easier to use. Electric caulking guns are powered by batteries or cords, offering even more convenience and precision in applying caulk. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, providing options for various levels of efficiency and control in caulking applications.</p></p>
