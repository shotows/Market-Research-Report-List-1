<p><p>An Intravascular Ultrasound System (IVUS) is a medical imaging technology that is used to visualize the interior of blood vessels in real-time. IVUS is commonly used in diagnosing and managing cardiovascular diseases such as atherosclerosis.</p><p>The IVUS market is experiencing significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
