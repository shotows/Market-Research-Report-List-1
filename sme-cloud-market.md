SaaS (Software as a Service) focuses on providing software applications through the cloud, allowing businesses to access and use them through the internet. IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service) provides virtualized computing resources like servers and storage, eliminating the need for physical infrastructure. PaaS (Platform as a Service) offers a platform for developing, testing, and deploying applications without the hassle of managing underlying infrastructure.
