This technology offers continuous charging, eliminating the need for frequent stops to charge the vehicle. Stationary Wireless Charging Systems, on the other hand, involve parking the vehicle over a charging pad to wirelessly receive power. Both systems aim to provide convenience and efficiency for electric vehicle users by making the charging process seamless and hassle-free.</p></p>
