<p><p>Heavy duty coatings are available in various types based on the solvents used in their formulation. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents to dissolve the resin and facilitate application. Water-based coatings use water as the primary solvent, making them environmentally friendly and low in VOCs. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and then heated to form a hard finish. Each type of coating offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and marine.</p></p>
