<p><p>Air wire hoists are available in three main market types: hydraulic, electric, and hybrid. Hydraulic hoists are powered by hydraulic fluid, which provides strong lifting capabilities and precise control. Electric hoists use electricity to power the hoisting mechanism, making them a popular choice for indoor applications. Hybrid hoists combine elements of both hydraulic and electric hoists to offer a versatile solution with a balance of power and control. Each market type caters to different lifting needs and environments.</p></p>
