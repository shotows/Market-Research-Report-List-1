<p><p>Pancreatic stents are small tubes inserted into the pancreatic duct to maintain its patency and facilitate drainage of fluids. There are two main types of pancreatic stents: plastic and metal. Plastic pancreatic stents are commonly used due to their flexibility and ease of removal. They are usually temporary and are often preferred in cases of benign pancreatic diseases.
