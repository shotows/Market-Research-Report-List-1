<p><p>PPS Composite materials can be classified into two main types: Cross-linked Type and Linear Type. Cross-linked Type PPS Composite materials contain strong chemical bonds between polymer chains, resulting in enhanced mechanical properties and thermal stability. Linear Type PPS Composite materials, on the other hand, have a more flexible molecular structure, offering better processability and moldability. Both types have unique properties and are used in various industries for applications requiring high performance and durability.</p></p>
