<p><p>Ice melter markets can be categorized into two main types: liquid ice melters and solid ice melters. Liquid ice melters are typically in the form of liquid solutions that are sprayed or poured onto icy surfaces to quickly melt the ice. Solid ice melters, on the other hand, are granular or pelletized products that are spread by hand or with a spreader. Both types of products are designed to efficiently and effectively melt ice and snow, providing safety for pedestrians and drivers during winter weather conditions.</p></p>
