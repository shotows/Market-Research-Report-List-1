<p><p>Specialty beverage stabilizers market types include Polivinylpolypyrolidone (PVPP), Silica Gel, Bentonite, Tannic Acid, Agarose, Enzymes, and others. PVPP is used for stabilizing beer and wine by removing polyphenols. Silica Gel absorbs water and prevents clumping in powdered drink mixes. Bentonite is a natural clay used for protein stabilization in wine. Tannic Acid is used for stabilizing fruit juices and wine. Agarose is a gelling agent for thickening beverages. Enzymes are used for clarifying and stabilizing beverages.</p></p>
