<p><p>The EGR Valve Market consists of two main types: Mechanical and Electronically Controlled. Mechanical EGR valves operate by using a vacuum created by the engine to control exhaust gas flow, while Electronically Controlled EGR valves utilize sensors and an electronic control unit to regulate exhaust gas recirculation. The Mechanical EGR valves are more traditional and usually found in older vehicles, while Electronically Controlled EGR valves provide more precise control and are commonly used in modern vehicles to meet stricter emissions regulations.</p></p>
