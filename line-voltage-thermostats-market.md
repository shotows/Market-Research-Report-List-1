<p><strong>The Line-Voltage Thermostats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pole Wiring</li><li>Double Pole Wiring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Line-voltage thermostats can be classified into two main market types based on their wiring: single pole wiring and double pole wiring. Single pole wiring thermostats are designed to control only one live wire, making them suitable for controlling one heating device. On the other hand, double pole wiring thermostats are capable of controlling both the live and neutral wires, enabling them to control multiple heating devices simultaneously. Each market type caters to specific heating needs and installation requirements, providing consumers with options to suit their preferences.</p></p>
