<p><strong>The Microneedle Drug Delivery Patches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dissolving Type</li><li>Insoluble Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microneedle drug delivery patches come in two main types: dissolving type and insoluble type. Dissolving type patches contain microneedles made from materials that dissolve once inserted into the skin, releasing the drug directly into the bloodstream. On the other hand, insoluble type patches have microneedles that remain in the skin after drug delivery, providing sustained release of the medication. Both types offer advantages in terms of controlled drug delivery, ease of use, and reduced risk of needlestick injuries.</p></p>
