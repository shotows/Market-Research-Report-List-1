<p><p>Trap primers are devices used in plumbing systems to prevent the evaporation of water in floor drains and traps. The market for trap primers can be categorized into four types: Continuous Flow, which provides a constant flow of water to the trap; Pressure Drop Activated, which releases water when pressure drops in the system; Flush Valve Operated, which activates when a flush valve is opened; and Electronic, which uses sensors to detect when water is needed in the trap.Each type offers unique advantages for maintaining proper drainage and preventing odors in plumbing systems.</p></p>
