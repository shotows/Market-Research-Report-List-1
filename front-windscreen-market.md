<p><strong>The Front Windscreen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front windscreen market types include laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass is made by bonding two layers of glass with a plastic interlayer, providing a strong barrier against impact and shattering. This type of windscreen is commonly found in vehicles for safety reasons. On the other hand, tempered glass is created by heating and cooling the glass rapidly to increase its strength. While tempered glass is less prone to shattering, it does not offer the same level of impact resistance as laminated glass.</p></p>
