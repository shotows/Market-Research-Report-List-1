<p><p>Dispensary POS System market types include single screen and dual screens. A single screen system consists of a single monitor that displays both the customer-facing interface and the back-end management functions. This type is suitable for small-scale dispensaries with limited space. On the other hand, dual screens have separate monitors for customers and employees. The customer-facing screen enables them to view product details and make selections, while the employee screen manages inventory, sales data, and other backend operations. Dual screens are ideal for larger dispensaries with higher customer flow, improving efficiency and customer service.</p></p>
