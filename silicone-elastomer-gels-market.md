<p><p>Silicone elastomer gels can be categorized into volatile and non-volatile types based on their composition. Volatile silicone elastomer gels contain ingredients that evaporate quickly when exposed to air, leaving behind a smooth and dry finish on the skin. On the other hand, non-volatile silicone elastomer gels consist of ingredients that do not evaporate easily, providing longer-lasting hydration and a more emollient feel. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and skin types, offering a variety of benefits in the beauty and personal care industry.</p></p>
