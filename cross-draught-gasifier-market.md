<p><p>Cross-draught gasifiers come in two main types: single stage and multiple stage. Single stage gasifiers have a simpler design and operate by passing the biomass feedstock through a single combustion zone. On the other hand, multiple stage gasifiers have a more complex design with several combustion zones, allowing for more efficient conversion of biomass into syngas. Both types of gasifiers are used in various industries for the production of biofuels and chemicals.</p></p>
