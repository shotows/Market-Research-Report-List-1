<p><p>Inguinal hernia graft market consists of two main types: direct inguinal hernia graft and indirect inguinal hernia graft. Direct inguinal hernia graft is used to repair a specific type of hernia that occurs when tissue or intestines push through a weak spot in the abdominal wall in a straight path. On the other hand, indirect inguinal hernia graft is used to repair a different type of hernia that occurs when tissue or intestines push through the inguinal canal, causing a bulge in the groin area.</p></p>
