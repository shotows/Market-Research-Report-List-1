<p><p>Cardiovascular Disease Monitoring and Diagnostic Devices Market refers to the market for devices used to monitor and diagnose cardiovascular conditions. ECG Systems record and analyze the electrical activity of the heart. Event Monitors are portable devices that record heart activity during specific events. Holter Monitors are portable devices that continuously record heart activity for 24-48 hours. Implantable Loop Recorders monitor heart activity over longer periods, implanted under the skin. ECG Management Systems are software platforms that allow storage, analysis, and sharing of ECG data for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
