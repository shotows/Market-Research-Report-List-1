<p><p>Industrial-use nitrile gloves are commonly categorized into two types: powdered and powder-free gloves. Powdered nitrile gloves have a cornstarch powder added to reduce friction and make them easier to put on and take off. On the other hand, powder-free nitrile gloves do not have any added powder, making them less likely to cause allergies and contamination. Both types are widely used in industrial settings where protection against chemicals, oils, and other substances is needed.</p></p>
