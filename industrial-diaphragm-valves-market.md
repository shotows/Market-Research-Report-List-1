<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Motorized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial diaphragm valves are commonly classified into three types based on their actuation method: manual, pneumatic, and motorized. Manual diaphragm valves are operated by hand, making them simple and cost-effective. Pneumatic diaphragm valves use compressed air to actuate the valve, providing faster and more precise control. Motorized diaphragm valves are operated by an electric motor, offering automated control and remote operation capabilities. Each type of valve has its own advantages and is chosen based on factors such as cost, precision, and ease of operation.</p></p>
