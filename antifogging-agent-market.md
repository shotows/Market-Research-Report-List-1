<p><strong>The Antifogging Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outside Antifoggant</li><li>Within Antifoggant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antifogging agent market can be categorized into two main types: outside antifoggants and within antifoggants. Outside antifoggants are applied to the external surface of a material to prevent fogging, such as on eyeglasses or car windshields. Within antifoggants are incorporated into the material itself, providing long-lasting fog prevention from the inside out. Both types serve the same purpose of maintaining clarity and visibility in various applications, but differ in their method of application and effectiveness.</p></p>
