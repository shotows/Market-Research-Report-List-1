<p><p>Optical release film is categorized into three main types based on thickness: below 30 um, 30 um-50 um, and above 50 um. Below 30 um films are ultra-thin and offer high surface smoothness and transparency, ideal for applications requiring precise and delicate release. Films in the 30 um-50 um range provide a balance between flexibility and strength, suitable for various industrial and commercial uses. Above 50 um films are thick and durable, offering excellent protection and handling for heavy-duty applications in industries such as electronics and packaging.</p></p>
