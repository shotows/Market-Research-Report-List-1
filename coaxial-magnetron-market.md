<p><p>A Coaxial Magnetron is a type of vacuum tube that generates microwave radiation. It consists of concentric cylinders, with an anode in the center and a cathode surrounding it. When a high voltage is applied between the cathode and anode, electrons are accelerated towards the anode, causing them to spiral in the magnetic field present in the device. This spiral motion results in the generation of microwave radiation that can be used in various applications such as radar systems, industrial heating, and medical equipment.</p><p>The Coaxial Magnetron Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
