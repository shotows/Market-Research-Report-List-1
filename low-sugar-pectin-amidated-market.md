<p><p>Low Sugar Pectin Amidated is available in two main types, High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin and Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin. HM Pectin requires a high sugar content to form a gel, making it suitable for traditional high-sugar jams and jellies. On the other hand, LM Pectin can form a gel with low sugar or even no sugar, making it ideal for low-sugar or sugar-free products. Both types offer different functionalities and applications based on sugar content and desired texture.</p></p>
