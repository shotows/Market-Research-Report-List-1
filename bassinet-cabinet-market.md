<p><p>Bassinet cabinet market is divided into two main types: closed bassinet cabinet and open bassinet cabinet. Closed bassinet cabinets have a solid structure with doors or drawers to provide privacy and security for the baby. They are suitable for parents who prefer a more enclosed space for their baby while sleeping. On the other hand, open bassinet cabinets have a more open design without doors or drawers, allowing for easier access to the baby. They are preferred by parents who want a more open and accessible sleeping space for their baby.</p></p>
