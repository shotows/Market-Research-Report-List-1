<p><p>The Invisalign suspender market comprises three main types: lingual suspenders, clear aligners, and ceramic suspenders. Lingual suspenders are attached to the back of the teeth, making them virtually invisible from the front. Clear aligners are custom-made, transparent trays that gradually shift teeth into alignment. Ceramic suspenders are similar to traditional braces but use tooth-colored or clear brackets to blend in with the teeth. Each of these market types offers discreet teeth straightening options, allowing individuals to achieve desired results without the visibility of traditional metal braces.</p></p>
