<p><strong>The Gear Reduction Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Offset Gear Reduction Starter</li><li>Planetary Gear Reduction Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gear Reduction Starters come in two main types: Offset Gear Reduction Starters and Planetary Gear Reduction Starters. Offset Gear Reduction Starters use an offset design to help reduce the size and weight of the starter motor, making it easier to fit into tight spaces in the engine compartment. On the other hand, Planetary Gear Reduction Starters use a planetary gear system to provide a higher torque output, allowing for more power to be delivered to the engine during start-up. Both types of gear reduction starters are popular in the automotive industry for their efficiency and performance benefits.</p></p>
