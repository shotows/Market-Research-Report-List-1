<p><p>Lyme Disease Diagnostic Devices Market offers various types of tests to detect Lyme disease. Serological tests analyze blood samples to identify specific antibodies produced by the body in response to the infection. Urine antigen tests detect proteins released by the bacteria in urine samples. Lymphocytic transformation tests measure a patient's immune response to the infection. Immunofluorescent staining uses fluorescent dyes to label the bacteria for detection. Nucleic acid tests identify the genetic material of the bacteria in body fluids or tissues. These tests aid in the accurate diagnosis and management of Lyme disease.</p></p>
