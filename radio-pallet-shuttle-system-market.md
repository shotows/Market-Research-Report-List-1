<p><p>The Radio Pallet Shuttle System market includes two main types: LIFO (Last In, First Out) and FIFO (First In, First Out). LIFO type is designed to retrieve the last item stored first, making it suitable for goods with expiration dates or low turnover. FIFO type, on the other hand, retrieves the first item stored first, which is beneficial for high-turnover goods or those that require quick processing. These market types offer different solutions to meet various storage and logistics needs in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
