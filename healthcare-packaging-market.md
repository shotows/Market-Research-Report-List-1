<p><strong>The Healthcare Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of packaging in the healthcare industry, namely primary packaging and secondary packaging. Primary packaging refers to the immediate packaging that comes into direct contact with the healthcare product, such as vials, ampoules, or blister packs. It ensures the product's integrity and safety during storage, transport, and use. On the other hand, secondary packaging is the outer packaging used to group and protect primary packaged products, like cartons or boxes. It provides additional barriers and labeling, facilitating handling, identification, and distribution of these healthcare products.</p></p>
