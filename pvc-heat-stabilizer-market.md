<p><p>PVC heat stabilizers are used to prevent the degradation of PVC materials when exposed to heat. There are different types of PVC heat stabilizers such as pastes, powders, prills, and others. Pastes are a mixture of stabilizers and plasticizers in a paste form, while powders are finely ground stabilizers that can be easily mixed into PVC formulations. Prills are small spherical beads of stabilizers, and others refer to different forms and compositions of stabilizers not categorized into the above three types. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the PVC heat stabilization market.</p></p>
