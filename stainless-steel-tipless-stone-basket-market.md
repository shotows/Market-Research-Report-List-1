<p><p>The stainless steel tipless stone basket market is categorized into three types: 3-Wire, 4-Wire, and 6-Wire. The 3-Wire basket consists of three wires that form a cylindrical structure with open mesh design, providing good maneuverability during stone extraction procedures. The 4-Wire basket, as the name suggests, contains four wires that increase the stability and durability of the basket. Lastly, the 6-Wire basket offers the highest level of stability and strength, making it suitable for challenging stone removal cases. These variations allow healthcare professionals to choose the most appropriate basket for specific cases and achieve better outcomes.</p></p>
