<p><p>Food service equipment refers to the various appliances and tools used in commercial kitchens and foodservice establishments for food preparation, storage, cooking, and serving purposes. The increasing demand for convenient, ready-to-eat food products has led to the expansion of foodservice establishments such as restaurants, hotels, and cafes, which in turn drives the demand for commercial refrigeration equipment. Additionally, the growing preference for fresh and healthy food among consumers has further fueled the market growth.</p><p>The increasing adoption of energy-efficient refrigeration systems is a major trend in the food service equipment (commercial refrigeration) market.
