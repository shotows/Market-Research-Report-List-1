<p><p>UA Curable Resins and Formulated Products market is categorized into different types including Oligomers, Monomers, Photoinitiators, Additives, and Others. Oligomers are larger molecules composed of repeating units, Monomers are smaller molecules that combine to form polymers, Photoinitiators kick off the curing process when exposed to UV light, Additives enhance performance or properties of the formulation, and Others include a variety of components such as pigments, fillers, and stabilizers. Each type plays a crucial role in the formulation and curing process of UV curable resins.</p></p>
