<p><p>An evaporative air conditioner, also known as a swamp cooler, is a cooling device that works by using the natural process of evaporation to cool the air. It involves the evaporation of water from a moistened pad or filter while passing warm outside air through it. As the water evaporates, it absorbs the heat from the air, resulting in cooler and more humid air being distributed into the surrounding space.</p><p>The evaporative air conditioner market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. Factors driving this growth include increasing energy efficiency concerns, rising demand for eco-friendly cooling solutions, and the ability of evaporative air conditioners to provide effective cooling in dry climates.
