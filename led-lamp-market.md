<p><p>LED lamp, also known as light-emitting diode lamp, is a type of lighting fixture that uses light-emitting diodes as a source of illumination. Another trend is the focus on sustainability, with manufacturers designing LED lamps with recyclable materials and minimal environmental impact.</p><p>Overall, the LED lamp market is poised for continued growth as consumers and businesses increasingly prioritize energy efficiency and sustainable practices.</p></p>
