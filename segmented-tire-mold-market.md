<p><ul><li>Top Open Tire Molds</li><li>Down To Ground Tire Molds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Segmented tire molds are used in the manufacturing of tires and are categorized into two types - Top Open Tire Molds and Down To Ground Tire Molds. Top Open Tire Molds are designed with an opening at the top, making it easier to remove the tire after it has been molded. On the other hand, Down To Ground Tire Molds do not have an opening at the top and require the tire to be removed from the bottom. Each type of segmented tire mold has its own advantages and is used based on specific manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
