<p><p>Rust removers can be categorized into three main types based on their acidity levels - Acidity Rust Remover, Alkaline Rust Remover, and Neutral Rust Remover. Neutral rust removers are gentle on surfaces and are used for light rust removal without causing damage. Each type of rust remover is specifically formulated to cater to different levels of rust and surface types.</p></p>
