<p><ul><li>Solar Tracker</li><li>Distributed Control System (DCS)</li><li>Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA)</li><li>Programmable Logic Controller (PLC)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar farm automation involves various types of systems to effectively control and monitor operations. Solar Tracker systems automatically adjust the position of solar panels to maximize energy collection. Distributed Control Systems (DCS) manage multiple process control systems in different locations. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems monitor and control solar farm operations. Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) automate specific tasks in solar farms. Other types of automation in the market include weather monitoring systems, remote monitoring services, and predictive maintenance solutions.</p></p>
