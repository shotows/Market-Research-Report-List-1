<p><p>The large molecule bioanalytical market includes ADME, PK, and PD markets. ADME (absorption, distribution, metabolism, elimination) studies focus on how a drug is processed within the body. PK (pharmacokinetics) studies analyze the drug's movement in the body, including absorption, distribution, metabolism, and elimination. PD (pharmacodynamics) studies examine the drug's effects on the body, such as mechanism of action and therapeutic effects. These markets play vital roles in drug development and are critical for ensuring the safety and efficacy of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
