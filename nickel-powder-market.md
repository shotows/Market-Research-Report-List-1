<p><p>Nickel powder is primarily available in two main types: Carbonyl Nickel Powder and Electrolytic Nickel Powder. Carbonyl Nickel Powder is produced through the gas-phase carbonylation process, resulting in high purity and unique particle morphology. On the other hand, Electrolytic Nickel Powder is manufactured using electrolysis techniques, yielding a more cost-effective option with lower purity levels. Both types of nickel powder are widely used in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive for their distinct properties and applications.</p></p>
