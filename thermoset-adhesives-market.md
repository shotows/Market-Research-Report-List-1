<p><p>Thermoset adhesives can be categorized into various types such as Phenolic Formaldehyde (PF) Resins, Phenolic Neoprene, Resorcinol Formaldehydes (RF), Polyesters, Polyimides, and Epoxy Resins. PF resins are known for their high heat resistance, while phenolic neoprene adhesives offer good chemical resistance. RF adhesives are valued for their water resistance properties. Polyesters are commonly used in laminating applications, and polyimides are known for their high-temperature stability. Epoxy resins are versatile and widely used in various industries for their strong bonding capabilities.</p></p>
