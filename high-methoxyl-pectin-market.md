<p><p>High Methoxyl Pectin Market Types refer to the various grades of pectin based on their setting times. Extra Rapid Set pectin sets quickly, making it ideal for products that need to gel rapidly. Rapid Set pectin also sets quickly but not as fast as Extra Rapid Set. Medium Rapid Set pectin sets at a moderate pace, while Slow Set pectin takes longer to gel. Extra Slow Set pectin has the longest setting time, making it suitable for products that require a slower gelling process.</p></p>
