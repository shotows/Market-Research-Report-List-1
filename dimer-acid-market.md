<p><p>Dimer acids are classified into three main types based on their production process: industrial dimer acids, distilled dimer acids, and hydrogenated dimer acids. Industrial dimer acids are produced using a chemical dimerization process, while distilled dimer acids are purified through distillation. Hydrogenated dimer acids undergo a hydrogenation process to improve their stability and performance. Each type of dimer acid has specific applications in industries such as adhesives, paints, coatings, and lubricants. The choice of dimer acid type depends on the desired characteristics for the end-use application.</p></p>
