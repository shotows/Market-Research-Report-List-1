<p><strong>The Clad Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded Clad Plate</li><li>Roll Bonded Clad Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clad Plate market consists of two main types: Explosion Bonded Clad Plate and Roll Bonded Clad Plate. Explosion Bonded Clad Plate is created by using explosives to bond two different metals together, resulting in a strong and durable material. Roll Bonded Clad Plate, on the other hand, is produced by pressing two metals together under high pressure, creating a metallurgical bond between them. Both types of Clad Plate are used in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace for their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
