<p><p>Gaseous air fresheners are available in different types such as ozone and negative ion markets. Ozone air fresheners work by releasing ozone molecules that eliminate odors and purify the air. On the other hand, negative ion air fresheners emit negatively charged ions that attach to pollutants in the air, causing them to fall to the ground and resulting in cleaner air. Both types of gaseous air fresheners provide effective solutions for eliminating unwanted odors and improving air quality.</p></p>
