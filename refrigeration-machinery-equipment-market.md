<p><p>The refrigeration machinery and equipment market includes various types of refrigeration machines. Compression refrigeration machines operate by compressing and expanding a refrigerant gas to cool the surroundings. Absorption refrigeration machines use a heat source, such as gas or electricity, to drive a chemical reaction that cools the system. Steam jet refrigeration machines utilize high-pressure steam to generate the cooling effect. These different types cater to distinct requirements and are used in various industries, such as food processing, healthcare, and industrial settings, to provide efficient cooling solutions.</p></p>
