<p><p>Medical X-ray devices market can be broadly categorized into two types: analog X-ray and digital X-ray. Analog X-ray devices use traditional film to produce images, while digital X-ray devices utilize electronic sensors to capture and store images digitally. Digital X-ray devices are gaining popularity due to their faster image processing, lower radiation doses, and easier image sharing capabilities. They offer greater efficiency and improved image quality compared to analog X-ray devices, making them the preferred choice for many healthcare facilities.</p></p>
