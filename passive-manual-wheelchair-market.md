<p><p>A passive manual wheelchair, also known as a self-propelled wheelchair, is a mobility device that is operated by the user through pushing the wheels with their hands. It is designed to provide mobility and independence to individuals who have limited or no strength in their lower extremities. These wheelchairs typically come equipped with large rear wheels and a set of smaller front wheels.</p><p>The passive manual wheelchair market is poised for significant growth in the coming years. Factors such as the rising prevalence of mobility impairments and disabilities, increasing aging population, and advancements in wheelchair technology are driving the market growth. The growing awareness regarding the importance of providing mobility aids to individuals with disabilities is also contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>Additionally, various initiatives taken by governments and healthcare organizations to promote inclusive mobility and improve accessibility for individuals with disabilities are positively impacting the market. The availability of different types of passive manual wheelchairs with advanced features, such as lightweight materials, improved ergonomics, and customizable options, is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>The market for passive manual wheelchairs is witnessing several trends.
