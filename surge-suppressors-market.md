<p><p>Surge suppressors are devices used to protect electrical equipment from power surges or spikes. There are three main types of surge suppressors: switch type, pressure limiting type, and flow or choke type. Switch type suppressors cut off the surge when it exceeds a certain voltage threshold. Pressure limiting type suppressors divert excess energy away from the equipment. Flow or choke type suppressors use inductors to limit the voltage spike. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
