<p><p>Coolant Switch Valve Market consists of two main types: 2 Way and 3 Way valves. 2 Way valves provide a simple on-off control for the flow of coolant, allowing it to either pass through or be blocked. On the other hand, 3 Way valves offer more control by diverting the flow of coolant between two different paths. Both types of valves play a crucial role in regulating the temperature of engines and various mechanical systems, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.</p></p>
