<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SUV ignition coil market consists of two main types: single-spark and multi-spark ignition systems. Single-spark systems produce a single spark per ignition cycle, while multi-spark systems produce multiple sparks during the same cycle. Single-spark systems are simpler and more cost-effective but may not provide as reliable ignition as multi-spark systems. On the other hand, multi-spark systems offer improved ignition efficiency and performance, making them a popular choice among SUV owners looking for enhanced engine power and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
