<p><p>The MDF Crown Moulding market can be divided into three main types: fire-retardant MDF, moisture resistant MDF, and general MDF. Fire-retardant MDF is treated to inhibit the spread of fire, making it suitable for buildings with strict fire safety requirements. Moisture resistant MDF is designed to withstand high humidity levels without warping or degrading, ideal for use in bathrooms and kitchens. General MDF is the standard option, versatile and cost-effective for a wide range of applications in construction and interior design.</p></p>
