<p><p>The Covid-19 vaccine market comprises different types of vaccines classified by their mechanism of action. DNA vaccines use genetically engineered DNA to stimulate an immune response. Non-replicating viral vector vaccines use a harmless virus to deliver Covid-19 genetic material to cells. Protein subunit vaccines contain fragments of the virus that trigger an immune response. RNA vaccines use genetic material to instruct cells to produce viral proteins. Other types include inactivated or live-attenuated vaccines that use weakened forms of the virus to induce immunity.</p></p>
