<p><p>Graphite Braided Packing generally comes in three different types: Dense Crystalline Graphite Packing, which is manufactured using high-quality raw materials for maximum performance; Flake Graphite Packing, which is made from expanded flexible graphite for excellent thermal conductivity and sealing properties; and Cryptocrystalline Graphite Packing, a cost-effective option that still offers good resistance to high temperatures and corrosive chemicals.
