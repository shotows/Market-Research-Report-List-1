Conical base tubes are designed for efficient pellet formation during centrifugation, while round bottom base tubes provide better visibility of samples. Flat bottom base tubes are ideal for stability and can stand upright on a flat surface. Each type caters to different laboratory needs and preferences, offering versatility and reliability for various scientific applications.</p></p>
