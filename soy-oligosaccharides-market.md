<p><strong>The Soy Oligosaccharides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soy oligosaccharides can be categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I soy oligosaccharides are smaller in molecular size and consist of short-chain oligosaccharides, such as fructooligosaccharides and galactooligosaccharides. They are commonly used as prebiotics in various food products and supplements to promote gut health. On the other hand, Type II soy oligosaccharides are larger in molecular size and consist of complex polysaccharides. They are typically used in industrial applications, such as in the production of biofuels and biomaterials.</p></p>
