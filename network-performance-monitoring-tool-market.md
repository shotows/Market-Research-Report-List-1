<p><p>The network performance monitoring tool market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based tools are hosted and accessed remotely, providing flexibility, scalability, and ease of deployment. They allow for real-time monitoring, analysis, and troubleshooting through a web-based interface. On-premises tools, on the other hand, are installed and operated locally within the organization's infrastructure, providing complete control and security over the monitoring process. They may require more resources for maintenance and upgrades but offer more customization options and may be preferred in highly-regulated industries.</p></p>
