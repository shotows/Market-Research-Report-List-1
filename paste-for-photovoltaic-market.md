<p><ul><li>Front Side Ag Paste</li><li>Rear Side Ag Paste</li><li>Rear Side Al Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front Side Ag Paste is used in photovoltaic cells to create the front contact grid, allowing for electron collection and conduction of electricity. Rear Side Ag Paste is used for the backside contact grid, enabling the flow of electrons to the rear surface. Rear Side Al Paste is an alternative to Rear Side Ag Paste, using aluminum instead of silver for cost-effective production. These market types cater to different needs in the photovoltaic industry, offering various options for efficient solar cell manufacturing.</p></p>
