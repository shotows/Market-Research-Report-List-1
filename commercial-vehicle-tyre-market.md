<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial tyres are a type of commercial vehicle tyre constructed with layers running radially across the tyre, providing improved tread wear, fuel efficiency, and a smoother ride. These tyres are commonly used in long-haul trucking and high-speed applications. Bias tyres, on the other hand, have layers that crisscross at an angle, providing better puncture resistance and load-carrying capacity. They are often used in off-road and agricultural applications. Both radial and bias tyres offer unique benefits suited to different commercial vehicle needs.</p></p>
