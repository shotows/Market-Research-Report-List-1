<p><p>The exhibition stand construction services market encompasses two main types: temporary exhibition and fixed exhibition. These stands are designed to be easily assembled and disassembled, allowing for flexibility and reusability. On the other hand, fixed exhibitions refer to static displays and installations found in museums, galleries, or permanent exhibition spaces.
