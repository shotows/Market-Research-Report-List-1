<p><p>Anti-venom market types include polyvalent anti-venom and monovalent anti-venom. Polyvalent anti-venoms are designed to treat bites from multiple species of venomous animals, providing broad-spectrum protection. Monovalent anti-venoms, on the other hand, target specific types of venom and are usually created for more common snake species. Both types are important in treating snake and spider bites, with polyvalent anti-venom being more versatile and monovalent anti-venom offering targeted treatment options.</p></p>
