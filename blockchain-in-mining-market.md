<p><strong>The Blockchain in Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Private Blockchain</li><li>Public Blockchain</li><li>Consortium Blockchain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Private Blockchain: A private blockchain is a closed network where only specific participants have access to the blockchain. It is controlled by a single organization and requires permissions to join. Private blockchains are used in industries such as healthcare or finance to maintain privacy and security.</p><p>Public Blockchain: A public blockchain is an open network accessible to anyone who wants to participate and contribute to the blockchain. No central authority controls it, and transactions are transparent for everyone to see. Bitcoin and Ethereum are examples of public blockchains.</p><p>Consortium Blockchain: A consortium blockchain is a hybrid between private and public blockchains. Multiple organizations collaborate to operate and control the blockchain, making it more decentralized than a private blockchain. Consortium blockchains are used in industries where multiple parties need to share information and make joint decisions, such as supply chain management.</p></p>
