<p><p>The smart temperature management market can be classified into three types: convection, conduction, and others. Convection-based temperature management involves the transfer of heat through the movement of fluids or gases, utilizing techniques like natural or forced convection. Conduction-based temperature management relies on the transfer of heat between objects that are in direct contact. Finally, the "others" category encompasses alternative methods such as radiation or induction heating. These diverse approaches enable efficient and precise temperature control in various applications, including electronics, automotive, industrial, and medical sectors.</p></p>
