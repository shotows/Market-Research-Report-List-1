<p><p>The processed glass market includes various types such as laminated glass, toughened glass, and others. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers of glass with an interlayer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) that holds the glass together upon impact, providing safety and security benefits. Toughened glass is heat-treated to increase its strength and resistance to breakage, making it ideal for applications requiring high durability. Other types of processed glass may include insulated glass, coated glass, or mirrored glass, each offering unique properties and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
