<p><p>Surgical Medical Laser Systems are categorized into four main types based on the laser technology used: Solid-state Laser Systems, Gas Laser Systems, Dye Laser Systems, and Diode Laser Systems. Solid-state lasers utilize a solid material as the lasing medium, gas lasers use gases such as CO2 or argon, dye lasers use organic dye solutions, and diode lasers use semiconductor materials. Each type of laser system offers unique advantages and applications in surgical procedures, making them essential tools in modern medical practices.</p></p>
