<p><p>Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM) are advanced imaging instruments used in various scientific fields. There are different types of SEMs available in the market, including W-SEM (standard SEM), FEG-SEM (field emission SEM), and FIB-SEM (focused ion beam SEM). The W-SEM utilizes a tungsten filament for imaging, while the FEG-SEM uses a field emission gun for higher resolution. FIB-SEM combines a focused ion beam for sample preparation and SEM for imaging, making it suitable for 3D imaging and nanofabrication applications.</p></p>
