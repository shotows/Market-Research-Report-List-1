<p><p>Transient Blocking Units (TBU) are devices designed to protect electronic systems from transient voltage spikes. There are two main types of TBUs on the market: Bidirectional TBUs, which provide protection from voltage spikes in both directions, and Unidirectional TBUs, which only protect against spikes in one direction. Bidirectional TBUs are commonly used in applications where voltage spikes can occur in both directions, while Unidirectional TBUs are used in applications where spikes are only expected in a single direction. Both types of TBUs help prevent damage to sensitive electronics caused by transient voltage spikes.</p></p>
