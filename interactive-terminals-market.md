<p><ul><li>Floor-Standing Interactive Terminals</li><li>Countertop Interactive Terminals</li><li>Wall-Mounted Interactive Terminals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interactive terminals market consists of three main types: floor-standing, countertop, and wall-mounted interactive terminals. Floor-standing interactive terminals are self-standing units often used in retail and hospitality industries, providing customers with a user-friendly interface for information, ordering, or interactive experiences. Countertop interactive terminals are compact units that are placed on countertops or desks, commonly seen in restaurants or banks, enabling customers to perform tasks such as payment or checking in. Wall-mounted interactive terminals are installed on walls and are commonly used in public spaces for informational purposes or touch-screen navigation.</p></p>
