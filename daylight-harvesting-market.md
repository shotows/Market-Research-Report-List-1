<p><strong>The Daylight Harvesting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Loop</li><li>Partial Open Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Daylight harvesting is a method used to optimize the use of natural light in buildings. In the closed-loop market, a sensor measures light levels and adjusts artificial lighting accordingly. In the partial open loop market, additional inputs like occupancy and time are considered alongside light levels for effective lighting control. Lastly, the open-loop market involves utilizing only manual inputs to adjust lighting systems. Each type offers varying degrees of automation and control, allowing for energy savings and improved lighting quality based on the availability of natural light.</p></p>
