<p><p>Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT) is a method of dialysis used to treat critically ill patients with acute kidney injury or renal failure. There are different types of CRRT, including hemodialysis and hemofiltration. Hemodialysis involves filtering out waste and excess fluids from the blood using a machine, while hemofiltration involves passing blood through a filter to remove toxins and balance electrolytes. Both methods are vital in managing patients with impaired kidney function.</p></p>
