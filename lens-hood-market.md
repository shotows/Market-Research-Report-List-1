<p><ul><li>Cylindrical Lens Hood</li><li>Taper Lens Hood</li><li>Petal Lens Hood</li><li>Square Lens Hood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lens hoods come in various types to effectively reduce glare and lens flare. Cylindrical lens hoods are simple and provide basic protection from stray light. Taper lens hoods have a wider opening at the front and are ideal for wide-angle lenses. Petal lens hoods have a unique shape that prevents vignetting on wide-angle lenses. Square lens hoods are versatile and can be adjusted to block light from different angles. Different market types cater to different needs and preferences of photographers.</p></p>
