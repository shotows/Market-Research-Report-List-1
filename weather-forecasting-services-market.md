<p><p>Weather forecasting services market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. Short-range forecasting: This type predicts weather conditions up to three days in advance. It helps individuals and businesses plan daily activities, such as outdoor events or travel arrangements.</p><p>2. Medium-range forecasting: This market segment provides weather predictions for a week to two weeks ahead. It assists industries like agriculture, transportation, and energy in making informed decisions related to operations, logistics, and resource allocation.</p><p>3. Long-range forecasting: This market encompasses weather predictions for several weeks to months in advance. It aids businesses and industries in long-term planning, such as crop planting, inventory management, and demand forecasting.</p></p>
