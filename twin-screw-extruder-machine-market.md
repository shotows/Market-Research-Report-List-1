<p><strong>The Twin-screw Extruder Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Co-rotating</li><li>Parallel Counter-rotating</li><li>Conical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Twin-screw extruder machines come in three main types: parallel co-rotating, parallel counter-rotating, and conical. Parallel co-rotating machines have screws that rotate in the same direction, providing high levels of torque and mixing capabilities. Parallel counter-rotating machines have screws that rotate in opposite directions, offering enhanced self-cleaning and material conveyance. Conical machines feature one screw inside the other, providing increased compaction and pressure. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the desired application and material being processed.</p></p>
