<p><p>Off-site light steel frame market includes three main types: H-shaped section, C-section, and others. H-shaped sections are commonly used for beams and columns due to their higher load-bearing capacity. C-sections are used for wall studs and are more cost-effective. Other types may include Z-sections and omega sections, which are used for specific applications such as roofing and floor joists. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the structural requirements and cost considerations of the project.</p></p>
