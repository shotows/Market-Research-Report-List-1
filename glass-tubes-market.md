<p><p>Glass tubes market can be categorized into three major types - Flint Glass, Borosilicate Glass, and Others. Flint Glass, also known as soda lime glass, is the most common type of glass tube used in various applications due to its low cost and ease of production. Borosilicate Glass, on the other hand, is known for its superior chemical and thermal resistance compared to other glass types. The Others category includes specialty glasses like quartz glass or lead glass, which are used in specific industries for their unique properties.</p></p>
