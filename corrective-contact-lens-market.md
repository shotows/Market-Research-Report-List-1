<p><p>Corrective contact lenses come in three main types: rigid contact lenses, soft contact lenses, and hybrid contact lenses. Rigid contact lenses are made of durable plastic material and provide clear vision and easy maintenance. Soft contact lenses are made of flexible, water-containing plastics for comfort and are popular for daily wear. Hybrid contact lenses combine features of rigid and soft lenses, offering the clarity of a rigid lens with the comfort of a soft lens. Each type caters to different individual preferences and needs for vision correction.</p></p>
