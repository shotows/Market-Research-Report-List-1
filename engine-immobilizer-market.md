<p><strong>The Engine Immobilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Electronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine immobilizers are security systems designed to prevent unauthorized access to a vehicle by disabling the engine. Mechanical immobilizers involve physical barriers like steering wheel locks or gearshift locks. On the other hand, electronic immobilizers use advanced technology such as transponders or smart keys to prevent the engine from starting without the correct signal. Both types offer effective protection against theft, with electronic immobilizers typically providing more sophisticated security features and convenience for the vehicle owner.</p></p>
