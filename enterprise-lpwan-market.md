<p><p>The enterprise LPWAN market is divided into two types: cellular-based LPWAN technologies and non-cellular LPWAN technologies. </p><p>Cellular-based LPWAN technologies use existing cellular networks to provide wide-area coverage with long-range, low-power connectivity. Examples include LTE-M and NB-IoT.</p><p>Non-cellular LPWAN technologies, on the other hand, have their own dedicated networks and protocols. These technologies, such as LoRaWAN and Sigfox, offer significant range and power efficiency advantages, making them suitable for various enterprise IoT applications. </p><p>Both types of LPWAN technologies cater to the specific connectivity needs of enterprises, enabling efficient and cost-effective IoT deployments.</p></p>
