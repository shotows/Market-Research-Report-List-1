<p><p>Oil and gas pipeline transportation refers to the infrastructural system used for the transportation of crude oil and natural gas from production sites to refineries, storage facilities, or distribution centers. These technologies help in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of pipelines by detecting leaks, monitoring pressure and flow rates, and providing real-time data for improved decision-making.</p><p>Another key trend is the development of cross-border and inter-regional pipeline networks to facilitate the international trade of oil and gas.
