<p><p>The pearl extract market consists of two main types: freshwater pearls and saltwater pearls. Freshwater pearls are formed in freshwater mollusks, such as mussels, and are known for their unique shapes and colors. On the other hand, saltwater pearls are formed in oysters that live in saltwater environments and come in more traditional round shapes. Both freshwater and saltwater pearls are sought after for their beauty and are used in various industries, including jewelry, cosmetics, and traditional medicine.</p></p>
