<p><p>Blood thaw bath market includes two types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic blood thaw baths are designed to partially automate the thawing process. They require some manual intervention, such as setting the desired temperature and monitoring progress. On the other hand, automatic blood thaw baths are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention. They have advanced features like pre-programmed thawing protocols and temperature control systems.
