<p><p>Kerosene lamps are a popular lighting option that come in various types. Wick lamps use a simple wick to draw fuel up and burn it, making them easy to use and maintain. Pressure lamps utilize a pressurized fuel tank to create a brighter, more consistent light. Both types of lamps are commonly used in areas with limited electricity or as a backup lighting source during power outages. Wick lamps are typically more affordable and portable, while pressure lamps are known for their powerful illumination.</p></p>
