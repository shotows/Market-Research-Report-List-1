<p><ul><li>OE</li><li>After Market</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High performance brake systems can be categorized into two main markets types: OE (Original Equipment) and Aftermarket. OE market refers to brake systems that are installed by the vehicle manufacturer during production. These systems are specifically designed and tailored for each vehicle to ensure optimal performance and safety. On the other hand, Aftermarket brake systems are replacement or upgrade options that are available for consumers to purchase separately and install on their vehicles. These systems may offer improved performance, durability, or aesthetics compared to OE options.</p></p>
