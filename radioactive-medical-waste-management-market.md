<p><p>Radioactive medical waste management refers to the safe disposal of waste generated from medical procedures involving radioactive materials. The market is divided into three types: low-level (LLW), intermediate-level (ILW), and high-level (HLW). LLW encompasses waste with low levels of radioactivity, ILW includes waste with higher levels of radioactivity, and HLW consists of waste with the highest levels of radioactivity. Each type requires specific handling and disposal methods to protect public health and the environment. The market for radioactive medical waste management caters to the specialized needs of each waste category.</p></p>
