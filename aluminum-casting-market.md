<p><p>Aluminum casting market is divided into different types: Die Casting, Permanent Molding Casting, Sand Casting, and Others. Die Casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold cavity under high pressure. Permanent Molding Casting utilizes a reusable mold for multiple castings. Sand Casting involves creating a mold by compacting sand around a pattern. The Others segment includes different casting techniques like investment casting and centrifugal casting. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various industries for producing aluminum parts.</p></p>
