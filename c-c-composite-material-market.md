<p><p>C-C composite materials are used in various industries due to their exceptional mechanical properties and high temperature resistance. Two common market types for C-C composite materials are Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process. In the CVD market, carbon fibers are deposited onto a substrate in a controlled environment, while in the Liquid Impregnation Process market, the carbon matrix is infiltrated with liquid resin to enhance the material's properties. Both processes result in high-quality C-C composite materials for different applications.</p></p>
