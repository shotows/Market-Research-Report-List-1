<p><p>Antifouling paint is a specialized coating applied to the hulls of boats and ships to prevent the growth of marine organisms such as algae, barnacles, and mollusks. Antifouling paint contains biocides that deter marine growth and help maintain the performance and efficiency of vessels.</p><p>The global antifouling paint market is expected to grow at a rapid pace, with a projected CAGR of 9.9% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Antifouling Paint Market consists of three main types: Eroding Antifouling, Hard Film Antifouling, and Others. Eroding Antifouling paints gradually wear away over time, releasing toxic substances to prevent marine growth. Hard Film Antifouling paints create a smooth, durable coating that prevents organisms from attaching to the surface. The Others category includes newer technologies such as silicone-based paints or copper-free alternatives. Each type of antifouling paint offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications in the marine industry.</p></p>
