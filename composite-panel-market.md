<p><p>A composite panel is a type of building material made up of two thin surface layers (or skins) bonded to a lightweight core material. These panels are widely used in the construction industry for applications such as wall cladding, roofing, and partitions. They offer several advantages, including high strength-to-weight ratio, thermal insulation, durability, and design flexibility.</p><p>The global composite panel market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period. The market is also witnessing a trend towards the use of innovative materials and technologies to enhance the performance and aesthetics of composite panels.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness about the environmental impact of traditional building materials is driving the adoption of composite panels, as they are more sustainable and eco-friendly. Overall, the composite panel market is poised for robust growth in the coming years.</p></p>
