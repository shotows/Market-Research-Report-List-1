<p><strong>The Flywheel Energy Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Based on Synchronous Motor Type</li><li>Based on Reluctance Motor Type</li><li>Based on Induction Motor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flywheel energy storage market can be categorized into three types based on the type of motor used. Firstly, the synchronous motor type utilizes magnets to create a rotating magnetic field, allowing energy to be stored in the flywheel. Secondly, the reluctance motor type uses the magnetic attraction between the rotor and stator, converting energy to mechanical motion and subsequently being stored in the flywheel. Lastly, the induction motor type utilizes electromagnetic induction, transferring electrical energy to mechanical energy stored in the flywheel. These three categories represent different approaches to energy storage in flywheel systems.</p></p>
