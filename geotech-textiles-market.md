<p><p>Geotech textiles market includes woven fabric, non-woven fabric, knitted fabric, and other types of fabrics. Woven fabric is made by weaving yarns together, providing high strength and durability. Non-woven fabric is formed by bonding fibers without weaving, offering superior filtration and drainage properties. Knitted fabric is produced by interlocking loops of yarn, creating flexibility and stretch. The "Others" category includes specialty fabrics such as braided fabrics or composite fabrics, catering to specific geotechnical applications. Each type of fabric serves different purposes in geotechnical engineering projects.</p></p>
