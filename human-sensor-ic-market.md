<p><p>Human Sensor IC market is segmented into Pyroelectric Infrared Sensors and Thermopile Sensors. Pyroelectric Infrared Sensors detect changes in infrared radiation emitted by a human body, while Thermopile Sensors measure temperature variations using the Seebeck effect. Both types of sensors are used in applications such as occupancy detection, security systems, and temperature measurement. Pyroelectric Infrared Sensors are more commonly used for motion detection, while Thermopile Sensors are preferred for temperature sensing applications due to their higher sensitivity and accuracy.</p></p>
