<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The GPS Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW Filters</li><li>BAW Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS filters market includes two types of filters: surface acoustic wave (SAW) filters and bulk acoustic wave (BAW) filters. SAW filters are based on the principle of utilizing sound waves to filter signals. They are known for their low insertion loss, small size, and low cost. On the other hand, BAW filters are based on the principle of utilizing vibrations in a quartz crystal to filter signals. BAW filters offer higher performance levels in terms of selectivity, power handling, and temperature stability, but they are comparatively larger and more expensive.</p></p>
