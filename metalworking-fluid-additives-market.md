<p><p>Metalworking Fluid Additives Market consists of various types of additives such as Lubricity Agents, Emulsifiers, Corrosion Inhibitors, Defoamers, Amines (Specialty amines), Biocides, and Others. Lubricity Agents are added to reduce friction and wear during metalworking processes. Emulsifiers help in maintaining stable emulsions of oil and water in the fluid. Corrosion Inhibitors prevent the metal surfaces from corroding. Defoamers help in reducing foam formation. Amines and Specialty amines are used for pH control and rust prevention. Biocides eliminate bacteria and other microbes present in the fluid.</p></p>
