Inulin is a type of dietary fiber that acts as a prebiotic, promoting the growth of healthy gut bacteria. Sugar alcohols, such as xylitol and erythritol, provide sweetness without the same impact on blood sugar levels as regular sugar. Other functional sugars include resistant starches and glycosides, which also offer health benefits when included in dietary supplements.</p></p>
