<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive CMOS Image Sensors (CIS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-pixel CIS (0-2M)</li><li>Mid-high Pixel CIS (2-5M)</li><li>High-pixel CIS (5-8M)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive CMOS Image Sensors (CIS) are categorized into three main types based on pixel resolution. Low-pixel CIS (0-2M) are commonly used for basic applications such as rear-view cameras. Mid-high Pixel CIS (2-5M) are more advanced and used for applications requiring higher image quality, like parking assistance systems. High-pixel CIS (5-8M) offer the highest resolution and are used for features like lane departure warning systems. Each type of CIS caters to different automotive imaging needs, offering varying levels of image clarity and detail.</p></p>
