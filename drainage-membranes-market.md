<p><p>Drainage membranes are materials used in construction and civil engineering projects to provide effective drainage and prevent water damage. These membranes are typically made of synthetic materials like HDPE, PVC, and PP, with dimpled surfaces that allow water to flow freely through while keeping soil and other particles out. They are commonly used in applications such as green roofs, landscaping, basements, roadways, and tunnels.</p><p>The global drainage membranes market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for efficient water management solutions in construction and infrastructure projects. The market is also influenced by factors such as rising urbanization, growing awareness about sustainable building practices, and stringent government regulations regarding water runoff and drainage systems.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the drainage membranes market is the development of eco-friendly and biodegradable materials to reduce environmental impact. Manufacturers are also focusing on innovations like self-adhesive membranes and advanced drainage solutions to cater to evolving customer needs. Additionally, the adoption of geotextiles and geocomposites in combination with drainage membranes is expected to further boost market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
