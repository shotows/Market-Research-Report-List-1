<p><strong>The Rodenticides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anticoagulants</li><li>Non-Anticoagulants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rodenticides are chemicals used to kill rodents such as mice and rats. There are two main types of rodenticides on the market: anticoagulants and non-anticoagulants. Anticoagulant rodenticides work by preventing blood clotting, leading to internal bleeding and eventual death. Non-anticoagulant rodenticides, on the other hand, work by targeting the nervous system or other vital organs of the rodents. Both types are commonly used for rodent control in various settings such as households, farms, and commercial spaces.</p></p>
