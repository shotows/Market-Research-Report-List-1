<p><p>The Inertial Navigation System (INS) market is segmented into four main types based on the technology used: Mechanical Gyro Technology, Ring Laser Gyro Technology, Fiber Optics Gyro Technology, and MEMS Technology. Mechanical Gyro Technology relies on rotating masses to provide navigation data. Ring Laser Gyro Technology uses the interference pattern of laser beams to determine orientation. Fiber Optics Gyro Technology utilizes the Sagnac effect in optical fibers for navigation. MEMS Technology uses micro-electromechanical systems for inertial sensing. These technologies cater to different needs and applications within the INS market.</p></p>
