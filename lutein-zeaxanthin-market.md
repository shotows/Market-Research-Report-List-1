<p><p>Lutein and Zeaxanthin are naturally occurring yellow pigments, known as carotenoids, found in various fruits and vegetables. Additionally, the rise in the prevalence of eye diseases, such as AMD and cataracts, further boosts the demand for these compounds. Moreover, the growing adoption of lutein and zeaxanthin supplements among health-conscious consumers has augmented market growth.</p><p>Several technological advancements and product innovations have propelled market growth by making lutein and zeaxanthin more accessible to consumers.
