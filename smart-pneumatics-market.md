<p><p>Smart pneumatics refer to the integration of pneumatic components with digital technology to enhance their functionality and efficiency. The market for smart pneumatics can be categorized into three main types: valves, actuators, and modules. Valves are responsible for controlling the flow of air or gas in a pneumatic system. Actuators convert energy into mechanical movements to perform tasks such as opening or closing valves. Modules provide connectivity and communication capabilities to facilitate data exchange and remote control of pneumatic devices. These technologies collectively contribute to the advancement of automation and industrial processes.</p></p>
