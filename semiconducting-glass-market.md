<p><p>Semiconducting glass market is divided into two types: Negative Type and Positive Type. Negative Type semiconducting glass contains excess electrons, making it a good conductor of electricity. Positive Type semiconducting glass, on the other hand, has a deficiency of electrons, making it a good insulator. Both types play a crucial role in various electronic applications, such as sensors, photovoltaic cells, and optoelectronic devices, by controlling the flow of electricity and facilitating the conversion of energy.</p></p>
