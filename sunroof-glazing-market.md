<p><p>Sunroof glazing market types include single, double, and triple Low-E glazing. Single glazing consists of one layer of glass, while double glazing has two layers with a space in between for insulation. Triple Low-E glazing adds an additional layer that includes a low-emissivity coating to reduce heat loss. Each type offers varying levels of insulation and energy efficiency. Customers can choose the best option based on their specific needs for light transmission, thermal insulation, and UV protection.</p></p>
