<p><p>CRT-P (Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemaker) market is segmented into three main types: Extracorporeal Portable Pacemaker, Implanted Pacemaker, and Semi-Buried Pacemaker. Extracorporeal Portable Pacemakers are device used externally to deliver electrical pulses to the heart. Implanted Pacemakers are surgically placed inside the body to regulate the heart's rhythm. Semi-Buried Pacemakers are partially implanted devices that sit just under the skin. Each market type caters to different patient needs and preferences, providing options for those requiring cardiac resynchronization therapy.</p></p>
