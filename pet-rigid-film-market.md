<p><p>PET rigid film market is categorized based on thickness into four types: <0.2mm, 0.2-1mm, 1-2mm, and >2mm. The <0.2mm segment is commonly used for packaging applications due to its flexibility and lightweight properties. The 0.2-1mm segment is preferred for thermoforming applications in industries like food packaging. The 1-2mm segment is suitable for applications requiring higher strength and durability. The >2mm segment is used for applications where rigidity and impact resistance are crucial, such as automotive and construction industries.</p></p>
