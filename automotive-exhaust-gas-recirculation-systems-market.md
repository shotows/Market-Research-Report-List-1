<p><p>Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation Systems market offers two types of EGR valves - Pneumatic EGR Valve and Electric EGR Valve. Pneumatic EGR Valve operates using air pressure to control the recirculation of exhaust gas, while Electric EGR Valve utilizes an electric motor to regulate the flow of exhaust gas. Both types of EGR valves are essential components in reducing emissions and improving fuel efficiency in vehicles. The choice between pneumatic and electric valves depends on the specific requirements of the vehicle and its engine.</p></p>
