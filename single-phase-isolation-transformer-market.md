<p><p>Single-Phase Isolation Transformers are primarily categorized into two types based on their function: Step-up Transformers and Step-down Transformers. Step-up Transformers increase the voltage level while decreasing the current, making them suitable for applications where high voltage is required. Step-down Transformers, on the other hand, lower the voltage level while increasing the current, making them ideal for applications where low voltage is needed. Both types provide electrical isolation between the input and output, ensuring safety and protection in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
