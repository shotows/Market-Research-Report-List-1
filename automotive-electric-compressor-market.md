<p><ul><li>Below 25 cc/r</li><li>25 ~ 40 cc/r</li><li>Above 40 cc/r</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive electric compressors are classified into three main types based on their displacement volume: Below 25 cc/r, 25 ~ 40 cc/r, and Above 40 cc/r. Compressors with a displacement volume below 25 cc/r are typically used in smaller vehicles or for specific applications where space is limited. Those in the 25 ~ 40 cc/r range are commonly found in mid-sized vehicles, offering a balance between efficiency and power. Compressors above 40 cc/r are designed for larger vehicles and heavy-duty applications that require higher levels of air compression.</p></p>
