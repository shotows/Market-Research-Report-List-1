<p><p>Aromatherapy oils are categorized into two main types - compound essential oil and unilateral essential oil markets. Compound essential oils are blends of two or more different essential oils, providing a complex and unique fragrance. On the other hand, unilateral essential oils are single oils extracted from a specific plant or herb, offering a pure and concentrated scent. Both types of aromatherapy oils cater to different consumer preferences, with compound oils offering diverse fragrances and unilateral oils providing a more straightforward and targeted scent.</p></p>
