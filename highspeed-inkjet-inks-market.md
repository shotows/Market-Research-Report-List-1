<p><strong>The Highspeed Inkjet Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Change Inkjet Inks</li><li>Solvent Based Inkjet Inks</li><li>Water Based Inkjet Inks</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Highspeed inkjet inks are classified into various types based on their composition and properties. Phase change inkjet inks use a solid wax or resin that changes phase to liquid upon heating, aiding in ink deposition. Solvent-based inkjet inks contain organic solvents that dissolve the ink pigments, resulting in a quick-drying ink. Water-based inkjet inks comprise water as a main solvent, providing eco-friendly and cost-effective printing solutions. Lastly, the "others" category includes specialty inks specifically designed for unique applications, such as UV-curable or latex-based inks. Each ink type offers distinct advantages and is tailored to meet specific printing requirements.</p></p>
