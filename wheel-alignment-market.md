<p><strong>The Wheel Alignment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thrust Alignment</li><li>Two-Wheel Alignment</li><li>Four-Wheel Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wheel alignment market types include thrust alignment, two-wheel alignment, and four-wheel alignment. Thrust alignment involves adjusting the rear wheels to be parallel with the front wheels. Two-wheel alignment focuses on adjusting the front wheels only, while four-wheel alignment adjusts all four wheels to ensure they are parallel and perpendicular to each other. Each type of alignment is important for maintaining proper vehicle handling, tire wear, and overall safety on the road. Customers can choose the type of alignment based on their specific vehicle needs and budget.</p></p>
