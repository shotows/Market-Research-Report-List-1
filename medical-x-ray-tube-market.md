<p><strong>The Medical X-Ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical X-ray tube market comprises two main types: the stationary anode medical X-ray tube and the rotating anode medical X-ray tube. The stationary anode tube is a fixed structure that generates X-rays by directing an electron beam at a tungsten target. It is commonly used in clinics and small hospitals for basic diagnostic imaging. In contrast, the rotating anode tube features a rotating disc that spreads the heat generated during X-ray production, enabling higher intensity imaging. It is typically used in larger medical facilities and specialized applications requiring higher resolution images.</p></p>
